h1. Presentation

@tex-templates@ is a collection of (La)TeX styles for the following universities:
- "Université de Technologie de Belfort-Montbéliard":http://www.utbm.fr
- "Université de Franche-Comté":http://www.univ-fcomte.fr
- "Université de Bourgogne":http://www.u-bourgogne.fr

The provided styles are for:
- PhD thesis (based on the tex-upmethodology class)
- Master thesis (based on the tex-upmethodology class)
- Reports (based on the tex-upmethodology class)
- HDR thesis (based on the tex-upmethodology class)
- Presentation styles (for Beamer)

An example of PhD thesis styles may be found at: http://www.multiagent.fr/ThesisStyle

h2. Installation

# Copy the content of the directory inside one of your @texmf@ directory, usually @/usr/share/texmf/tex/latex/upmethodology@ or @$HOME/.texmf@ on Unix operating systems.
# Update the LaTeX databases from the command line:

 $> mktexlsr
 $> update-updmap --quiet
